SQL query to answer the analytical question: "With which product did we achieve the highest turnover during the business year?"

SELECT 
  product_id,
  product_name, 
  sales_year,
  SUM(turnover) AS annual_turnover
FROM
  denor_view
GROUP BY
  product_id, product_name, sales_year
ORDER BY 
  annual_turnover DESC

LIMIT 1;
